Kampala Uganda

Kampala is the capital city of Uganda and the country’s main center for business, education, and culture. Known for its many hills, busy markets, and vibrant nightlife, Kampala blends modern city life with rich traditions. The city is home to important landmarks such as Kasubi Tombs and Makerere University, one of Africa’s oldest universities. People from different backgrounds live and work in Kampala, making it a lively and diverse city.
